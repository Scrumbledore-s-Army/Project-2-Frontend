Scandinavian Training Academy

Welcome to Scandinavian Training Academy, an Approved pilot Training Organisation (ATO) located in Copenhagen, Denmark, with multiple centers across Europe and the Middle East.

Welcome to Scandinavian Training Academy

We offer a range of Typerating courses and Proficiency training primarily focused on Airbus 320 and Boeing 737 and 787 – some of the world’s most prominent and widely used aircraft models.

We are dedicated to delivering unparalleled pilot training experiences and quality, that set the standard for excellence in the industry.

Quality, Flexibility and Personal Service

We are proud to deliver exceptional Typerating og Proficiency training experiences, and setting the industry standard for excellence.

With focus on quality, flexibility, and personal service, we prioritise meeting the aviation industry’s demanding standards of proficiency and skill.

To achieve thise high Typerating and Proficiency standards, our team comprises experienced aviation professionals, bringing decades of real-world expertise to both the classroom and the cockpit. Our instructors are not solely educators; they are also mentors, passionately committed to nurturing the next generation of skilled aviators.

Crafted with meticulous care, our Typerating and Proficiency training programs equip you with the skills and knowledge essential for a successful aviation journey. Regardless of whether you’re a seasoned pilot aiming to expand your horizons or a new aviator embarking on an aviation career, our comprehensive programs are tailored to address your unique needs.

Highly Qualified Instructor Team

Central to the Scandinavian Training Academy experience is our instructor team, who form the backbone of our organisation. With a genuine passion for teaching, they foster an environment that encourages growth and development for all pilots.

At Scandinavian Training Academy, you’re more than just a student enrolled in a Typerating or Proficiency Check course. We firmly believe that robust relationships are foundational to achieving success.

Despite our global reach, we’re not a large organisation where you are reduced to a mere number. Our commitment to personalised service is infused into every interaction – whether it involves responding to your queries, tending to your concerns, or celebrating your accomplishments.

Your success is our success, and we are with you all the way until you pass your Typerating or your Proficiency Check.

At Scandinavian Training Academy we make training simple!
